# 英文影子跟读｜2026-07-18

> 今日来源：名人演讲｜难度：A2–B1｜训练时间：约 12–15 分钟

## 一、今日声音

今天选的是 Steve Jobs 在斯坦福大学 2005 年毕业典礼上的演讲。他用自己年轻时旁听书法课、后来参与设计 Macintosh 字体的经历，说明很多选择在发生时看不出意义，回头看才会连成一条线。

这期不谈职业规划，而是练习一种更轻松的生活视角：允许自己保持好奇，先认真体验，再慢慢理解。

## 二、一句原声

> You can only connect them looking backwards.

**中文理解：** 你只有回头看时，才能把那些人生节点连接起来。

**出处：** Steve Jobs，Stanford University Commencement Address，2005 年 6 月 12 日。

**原声入口：** [斯坦福大学官方视频](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UF8uR6Z6KLc)  
**文本核验：** [Stanford Report 官方演讲稿](https://news.stanford.edu/stories/2005/06/youve-got-find-love-jobs-says)

## 三、背景故事

Jobs 在演讲的第一个故事里回忆，自己退学后因为兴趣旁听了一门书法课。当时这件事似乎没有实际用途；多年后，他参与设计 Macintosh，过去学到的字体、字距与排版知识却重新出现，影响了电脑的文字呈现。

这句话的重点不是“所有事情命中注定”，而是：当未来还不清楚时，我们可以先对真正吸引自己的事保持好奇；回头时，再判断哪些经验值得带走。

## 四、影子跟读短文

> 以下短文为本期原创练习稿，并非 Steve Jobs 演讲原文。建议先用设备朗读功能以 0.85 倍速播放，再逐步提高到正常速度。

### 自然阅读版

Sometimes life makes sense only after we look back. A small choice can feel unimportant today, yet become useful years later. You may read a book because the cover catches your eye. You may learn to cook because a friend invites you into the kitchen. You may take a different street and discover a quiet place that becomes special to you.

At the time, these moments do not form a clear plan. They are simply dots. We often want every dot to explain the future, but life rarely works that way. A better approach is to stay curious and pay attention. Choose things that teach you, wake you up, or bring honest joy. You do not need to prove that each interest will be useful.

Later, when you face a new problem, an old experience may return with new meaning. The book may give you the right words. The cooking lesson may teach you patience. The quiet street may remind you to slow down.

Looking back does not mean that every event was good or necessary. It means we can notice what we learned and decide what to carry forward. Keep collecting meaningful dots. Trust grows when you see that your path has already taught you how to take the next step.

## 五、中文理解

很多经历在当下只是零散的小事：偶然读的一本书、因为朋友学会的一道菜，或无意中走过的一条街。它们不一定立即产生价值，也不需要被证明“有用”。保持好奇、认真体验之后，旧经历可能在未来带来语言、耐心或新的视角。回望不是把所有事情美化，而是辨认自己学到了什么，并决定把什么带向下一段路。

## 六、发音与节奏

### 今日重点：内容词重读，功能词弱读

英语句子通常不会平均用力。名词、动词、形容词等内容词承担主要信息，要更清楚、更突出；can、to、a、the 等功能词通常更轻、更短。

### 跟读例子

1. A small **CHOICE** can feel unimportant **TODAY**.  
   can 轻读为 /kən/，重音放在 choice 和 today。

2. You may learn to **COOK** because a **FRIEND** invites you.  
   to 常弱读为 /tə/，不要逐词停顿。

3. Keep collecting meaningful **DOTS**.  
   句末 dots 要落稳；collecting 与 meaningful 之间保持连贯气流。

## 七、五个重点词语

### 1. look back /lʊk bæk/ 动词短语

**中文：** 回头看；回顾

**常见搭配：** look back on、never look back、look back with gratitude

**例句：** When I look back, I can see how much I learned.  
当我回头看时，我能发现自己学到了很多。

### 2. make sense /meɪk sens/ 动词短语

**中文：** 讲得通；变得有意义

**常见搭配：** make sense now、make sense of、it makes sense

**例句：** The experience did not make sense until years later.  
直到几年后，那段经历才显出意义。

### 3. curious /ˈkjʊriəs/ 形容词

**中文：** 好奇的；求知欲强的

**常见搭配：** stay curious、curious about、naturally curious

**例句：** Stay curious about places you do not know.  
对不了解的地方保持好奇。

### 4. pay attention /peɪ əˈtenʃən/ 动词短语

**中文：** 留意；专心

**常见搭配：** pay close attention、pay attention to、get someone's attention

**例句：** Pay attention to the moments that give you energy.  
留意那些让你产生能量的时刻。

### 5. carry forward /ˈkæri ˈfɔrwərd/ 动词短语

**中文：** 延续；把某物带到下一阶段

**常见搭配：** carry forward a lesson、carry an idea forward、carry forward into

**例句：** I want to carry this lesson forward into my daily life.  
我想把这个体会带进今后的日常生活。

## 九、开口迁移

**问题：** What is one small interest or experience from your past that became meaningful later?

**参考回答：**

A few years ago, I started taking long walks without a clear goal. At first, I only wanted some quiet time. Later, those walks helped me notice small details, organize my thoughts, and enjoy being alone. Looking back, the habit taught me to slow down and pay attention. I still take walks when life feels noisy. It is a simple experience, but I have carried the lesson forward.

**三个可迁移句型：**

1. At first, I only wanted to ___.
2. Looking back, it taught me to ___.
3. I did not see its value until ___.

**自检：**

- 是否说满 30 秒
- 是否使用至少两个重点表达
- 是否把内容词读得更清楚、功能词读得更轻
- 是否讲了“当时”和“后来”的变化

## 十、今日带走

> Let curiosity collect the dots; time will help you see the picture.

让好奇心先收集那些节点，时间会帮助你看见完整图景。
